How to use

  • Slide or tap your finger on the screen to adjust the brightness - the top is light and the bottom is dark. The edges of the screen are a "dead zone" (shown in darker colour here) to prevent accidental adjustments while holding your device.
  • Tap the top ? to open this screen.
  • Tap the bottom × to exit the app.
